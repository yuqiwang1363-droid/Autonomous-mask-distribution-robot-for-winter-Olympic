# Mechanical Design Overview

## Design Objectives

The mechanical system is designed to integrate multiple functional modules
within a compact mobile robot platform. Key objectives include:

- Reliable mask dispensing and recycling
- Safe human–robot interaction
- Ease of maintenance and module replacement
- Structural stability during omnidirectional motion

---

## Overall Mechanical Layout

The robot adopts a layered mechanical layout:

- Bottom layer: mobile platform with mecanum wheels and drive motors
- Middle layer: mask dispensing and recycling mechanisms
- Upper layer: sensing, interaction, and control components

This layout improves structural balance and simplifies subsystem integration.

---

## Mask Dispensing Mechanism

The mask dispensing mechanism is designed to deliver a single mask per interaction.

- Motor-driven roller mechanism controls mask output
- Inclined guide surface assists gravity-based delivery
- Infrared sensor detects successful mask release

The mechanism is modular and detachable, allowing quick replacement
or refilling of mask boxes.

---

## Mask Recycling and Disinfection Structure

- Dedicated inlet for used-mask collection
- Internal removable collection bin
- UV disinfection compartment integrated into the enclosure

This design minimizes secondary contamination and simplifies maintenance.

---

## Mobile Base and Structural Considerations

The mobile base uses a mecanum-wheel configuration to enable omnidirectional motion.

- Four independent drive units
- Symmetric structural layout for balanced load distribution
- Compact footprint suitable for crowded public environments

---

## Engineering Considerations

- Structural rigidity versus weight trade-off
- Accessibility for maintenance and refilling
- Safety during close human interaction
- Manufacturability using common fabrication methods

# 机械结构设计说明（中文版）

## 设计目标

本系统机械结构设计的主要目标包括：

- 实现口罩发放与回收的可靠运行  
- 保证人机交互过程中的结构安全性  
- 便于日常维护、补给与模块更换  
- 在全向移动过程中保持结构稳定性  

---

## 整体机械布局

机器人整体采用分层式机械布局：

- 底层：麦克纳姆轮移动底盘及驱动电机  
- 中层：口罩发放与回收机构  
- 上层：传感器、人机交互及控制单元  

该布局有利于系统集成与重心控制。

---

## 口罩发放机构设计

口罩发放机构用于实现单口罩、可控输出。

- 电机驱动胶轮控制口罩输送  
- 倾斜导向结构辅助口罩下滑  
- 红外传感器用于检测口罩是否成功发放  

该机构采用模块化设计，便于快速更换和维护。

---

## 口罩回收与消毒结构

- 设置独立的废弃口罩投入口  
- 内部配置可拆卸回收盒  
- 集成紫外线消毒结构以降低污染风险  

---

## 移动底盘与结构设计

移动底盘采用麦克纳姆轮结构：

- 四轮独立驱动  
- 对称结构设计保证载荷均衡  
- 适用于人员密集场所的灵活移动需求  

---

## 工程设计考虑

- 结构强度与整机重量之间的平衡  
- 维护与补给操作的便利性  
- 人机近距离交互时的安全性  
- 实际制造与装配可行性
